Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

Spaghetti with Marinara Sauce and Meatballs

Having trouble on what to make the family for super tonight? Look no further than the classic of spaghetti and meatballs. This very simple dish feeds a lot with a little and no real clean up afterwords. It is perfect for a simple good meal the whole family would enjoy.


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
    1. Wheat
    2. Grain
    3. Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pasta Sauce
    1. Marinara
    2. Vodka
    3. Pesto
    4. Alfredo
  • Meatballs
    1. Beef
    2. Pork
    3. Lamb
    4. Tofu

Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 350 degrees F
  2. Fill cooking pot about halfway full of water
  3. Bring water to boil on the stove
  4. Cover baking sheet with tinfoil, followed by placing meatballs evenly spaced covering the baking sheet
  5. Set timer for 25 minutes and put meatballs in oven and start timer
  6. Put 1 tbsp full of salt in boiling water followed by spaghetti
  7. Turn heat down to medium flame on the stove, and stir spaghetti with wooden spoon every minute and a half
  8. Cook spaghetti for 7 minutes and drain in collander placed in sink
  9. Dump spaghetti back into cooking pot along with the pasta sauce
  10. Once timer for the oven goes off, place the meatballs into the cooking pot with Spaghetti and Sauce
  11. Finished!!
