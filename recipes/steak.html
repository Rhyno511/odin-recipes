Steps to Cooking

  1. Dice up a quarter of an onion and 2-3 garlic cloves and put in cold skillet with half inch chunk of butter
  2. Lightly cover both sides of steak with salt and pepper and let sit in refridgerator
  3. Heat skillet up on medium-high heat and cover bottom with the melted butter and onions and garlic cooking, stirring around every 25 seconds or so
  4. Once butter is melted and skillet is hot, bring steak out of fridge and put it on the skillet
  5. Let cook for about 4-5 minutes or until the side of the steak is cooked about halfway thru, Then flip
  6. Let cook for another 5 minutes. Take steak off skillet and set on cutting board
  7. Let steak rest for about 8 minutes
  8. Cut and serve!!
